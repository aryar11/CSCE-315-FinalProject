Depending on how you plan on camping, the folllowing information might differ but here's our general camping checklist for you.

First, each individual camping needs a proper backpack, one that is rated to hold 30-50 liters is proper for overnight trips. As for the gear in your backpack, you'll need:

- Backpack with raincover
- Backpacking tent
- Sleeping bag
- Sleeping pad
- Headlamp with extra batteries


As for what to bring to prepare food, we got you covered. You'll need

- Backpacking Stove
- Fuel for Stove
- Dishes/Bowl
- Utensils
- Cup
- Soap
- Small towel
- Sollapsible water container
- Food sack


Make sure to plan your meals accoringly and pack for an extra day's supply of food. Energy bars and drinks are a good snack while on the move and plan your meals to be more on the side of a large snack. Water is the most important for survival and a good time. In addition to your water bottle, bring a water filter/purifier.

Once the essentials are packed, make sure you aquire the proper clothing to make yourself confortable. Always pack for unexpected weather.


- Moisture wicking underwear
- Mositure wicking t-shirt
- Quick drying pants
- Long sleeve shirt
- Lightweight jacket
- Hiking boots
- Wool socks
